We're thrilled to announce that **MetaSafe** has been selected as one of the prestigious **Hello Tomorrow Deep Tech Pioneers** for 2026!

### A Global Recognition of Innovation

Out of **more than 4,800 applications** from **108 countries** worldwide, our revolutionary dissipative metamaterial technology distinguished itself as a leading light in deep tech innovation. This recognition validates our groundbreaking approach to shock absorption and energy dissipation.

### What This Means

Being selected as a Deep Tech Pioneer represents:

- **Global Validation** of our metamaterial technology's potential to transform protective systems
- **Elite Recognition** among the world's most promising deep tech ventures
- **Exclusive Access** to Hello Tomorrow's dynamic community and year-round opportunities
- **Advancement** to Stage 2 of the Hello Tomorrow Challenge

### Next Steps: Amsterdam Finals

As Deep Tech Pioneers, we've advanced to the second stage of the Hello Tomorrow Challenge. **Finalists will be announced at the end of March 2026**, with the ultimate winners pitching on stage during the **Hello Tomorrow Challenge Finals in Amsterdam on June 11-12, 2026**.

### About Our Technology

Our patented yield-buckling mechanical metamaterial architecture represents a paradigm shift in protection technology. Unlike traditional materials that trade stiffness for energy absorption, MetaSafe's technology achieves both - delivering materials that are **100 times stiffer** and **20 times more dissipative** than current solutions.

This recognition from Hello Tomorrow reinforces our mission to bring revolutionary protection technology from laboratory research to real-world applications, starting with next-generation battery enclosures for electric vehicles.
